Our Story

Chicken Box was an idea I thought of when I was in jail thinking to myself about what I could do to make an honest living. I thought about many different options and I remembered my passion: cooking. I like cooking and thought "why not do something that I’m going to enjoy and put my heart into". So when I came out I started Chicken Box. Promoting it on social media and spreading the word through friends and family. We have been very successful and have been getting a lot of good feedback.

Our aim is to get the word spread all across London and (eventually) open a dine-in restaurant where people can come in and enjoy our great box and leave with full bellies and have a beyond satisfactory experience.
