The Masjid Website Program

Why is your website important?

A Masjid’s website is the heart of all of the information that a person needs to find. It needs to have a nice aesthetic and functionality to increase the traffic to website.

If your website doesn’t get much traffic then you’re missing out on these things:

  • An easy way to get donations
  • A clean interface for people to see information on events
  • Better reach to parents who could enrol their kids at your madrassah
  • A simple way to inform the people about upcoming events

The problem with masjid websites

Websites that are made for Masaajid usually have the following problems:

  • They look outdated
  • They look bad on mobiles
  • They have old information which needs changing
  • The salah timetable is broken
  • Nobody visits them

The solution

I’m a freelance web developer. I noticed this problem amongst Masaajid. The people who run the houses of Allah want the youth to come in and benefit from the classes and the duroos that take place but they have a bad online presence which includes a bad website, bad social media which all needs fixing.

To bridge the gap between the information that the organisers hold and the people who pray at the Masjid, I’ve decided to discount my services and offer a website to anyone who runs a Masjid and wants a website for that Masjid.
